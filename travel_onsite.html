Travel

The Venue

Our venue is Holly Farm, located at 9200 Carmel Valley Rd, Carmel, CA 93923

The closest major airports to the venue are:

San Francisco International Airport (SFO) ~2Hr Drive

San Jose International Airport (SJC) ~1.5hr Drive

Rentals

All major rental car providers have locations near both SFO and SJC.

In addition to parking at your accomodations, you are also welcome to park at The Holly Farm for as long as you’d like from Thursday morning through Sunday afternoon.

Lodging

You may either rent a house or stay in a hotel in the area. Transportation will be available to/from the ceremony and reception between the hours of 10:00PM and 1:00AM.

Please reach out to us with questions at any point!

Hotels

CARMEL RIVER INN

10 min. drive to Holly Farm, rates range from ~ $165 - 215/night.

Cozy cottages and inn guest rooms tucked into 10 acres of gardens, forests, and meadows. Inn guest rooms feature king or double rooms. Cottages vary in size: studios, studios w/ kitchenettes, and 2 bedrooms. Pet friendly.

CARMEL VALLEY LODGE & RESORT

8 min. drive to Holly Farm, rates range from ~ $299 - 520/night

Set on 3+ acres of lush flowers & fruit trees. Walkable to Carmel Valley Village, which features restaurants, shops and 27+ wine tasting rooms. Pool on-site. Cottages vary in size: king rooms and two bedroom cottages. Pet friendly.

Please call the hotel at 000-000-0000 to book your rooms. Note that we don’t technically have a “room block” here, so there may be confusion if you mention a block—the rooms are first come, first served, but please feel free to book here!

Houses
