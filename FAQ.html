Frequently Asked Questions

Venue Accomodations

Where will I be staying?

You will be staying in one of four houses on the property. We will show you to your specific house when you arrive!

Please be aware that while there will be a bed for you, there is not enough space for everyone to have a private room and some sleeping spaces are in living room areas.

If you would prefer to have your own room somewhere off property, please reach out to Maya and Jade. We'll see what we can do!

What is included in each house?

Each house is equipped with a washer/dryer and a fully functional kitchen. We will be making a Costco run to stock up on basics, but please feel free to bring any food you would like!

General

Will the wedding be indoors or outdoors?

All events, including the ceremony and reception, will take place outdoors. The venue is well-shaded and most of the paths are dirt or grass - so please plan footwear accordingly! See the Attire section for more info.

What is the Friday Welcome Party?

Come hang out with us the night before our ceremony! We'll be enjoying a casual evening of tacos, margaritas, and lawn games on the Windmill Lawn at Holly Farm.

There is no pressure to attend this event, but please make sure to RSVP if you'll be joining us.

Can I bring a plus one?

Yes, but we require that plus-ones are limited to significant others only. Just make sure to include them on your RSVP!

Are pets allowed?

No. There is a strict no-pets policy at the venue.

Can I bring a gift?

Your presence at our ceremony is more than enough!

Also, in lieu of a guest book, we are asking for guests to write down their favorite recipes. You should have received an index card along with your invitation, and there will be a box to collect them at the ceremony.

If you still want to bring a gift, you can find information about our registry here.

Logistics

How do I RSVP?

Please RSVP using the form here. You will be asked to enter your email address, and you will receive a confirmation email once you have RSVPd.

If you need to modify your RSVP, please reach out to Maya and Jade before submitting a new form.

How do I get there?

For travel and lodging info, check out the Travel Info page.

When should I arrive?

The recommended arrival time on Thursday, July 30 is between 3 and 6, as we will be having an informal dinner at Holly Farm around 7.

Attire

What is the dress code?

For the ceremony and reception, semi-formal attire is requested. We want everyone looking their best, but we encourage personal expression and unique looks!

For the welcome party on Friday evening, casual attire is recommended. Lawn games will be played, so shoes and clothes that allow movement may give you a competitive advantage...

Please note that all event spaces, including dinner and reception, are outdoors. We expect the weather to be beautiful, but it may cool down at night. Consider bringing an extra layer just in case!
